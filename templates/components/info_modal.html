🌟 Application Features

Overview

Welcome to the Global Meteorite Specimens & Impact Craters Visualization. This interactive tool allows you to explore meteorite landings recorded by NASA and discover impact craters around the world - in both 2D and 3D!

Features:

  • 🔄 Navigation: Use mouse or touch controls to rotate, zoom, and pan around the globe.
  • 🔍 Search: Fly to a specific location using the search bar in the Options menu.
  • ⚙️ Filters: Adjust filters like year, mass, diameter, age, class, and target rock type in the Options menu to refine the displayed data.
  • 👁️ Show/Hide Data: Toggle meteorites and impact craters visibility using the checkboxes in the Options menu.
  • 🎨 Color Schemes: Customize color schemes for meteorites and impact craters in the Key menu.
  • 📜 Legends: View legends for meteorite and crater color schemes in the Key menu.
  • 🔗 Clustering: Enable or disable clustering of meteorite markers to manage display density.
  • 🏆 Top Bars: Explore top meteorites and impact craters at the bottom of the screen.

Data Sources:

  • 🌠 NASA Meteorite Landings Dataset
  • 💥 Impact Crater data from Dr. Matthias Ebert
